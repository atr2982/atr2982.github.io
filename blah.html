Destiny Character Explorer

Site Description Below

Destiny Character Explorer was created to help a player keep track of items and stats on their characters while playing. Because of only being able to log into one character at a time, this makes it hard to manage items on all your characters. DCE eliminates that problem and provides a single place to view all characters.
